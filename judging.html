Judging Criteria

Teams who fit the following criteria will be eligible to present and win prizes.

Pitch Team Criteria:

Employees can submit a pitch before the main Hack to the Future event. Pitch submissions will be shortlisted by a panel of judges to demo their idea at the Hack kick off event, encouraging Hack teams to work on your idea during Hack to the Future.

  • At least 3 people on the team.
  • Exactly 1 minute to pitch.
  • Submit a completed 1 minute pitch deck and/or video by Wednesday, August 8, 11:00am ET.
  • There will be one prize awarded for Best Overall Pitch.

Hack Team Criteria:

  • At least 4 people from 4 different departments on the team.
  • Must build and present a coded prototype.
  • Submit your hack into one category.
  • Exactly 2 minutes to demo.
  • Submit your team and demo details by Friday, August 10 at 8:00am ET

You can recruit as many people as you like for your team!

Departments are defined as:

  • Content (Acquisition, Researchers, Editors, Contributor Support)
  • Data Science & Analytics
  • Global Customer Care
  • Marketing (Creative, Content Marketing, SEO, Product Marketing, Sales Marketing, Media, CRM, Communications, Events)
  • Operations (Finance, Accounting, Legal, HR, Facilities)
  • Product (Product Managers, UX)
  • Sales (AE, AM, SAE)
  • Platform Solutions
  • Tech (Developers, Engineers, Tech Ops, Infrastructure, QA, BI, IT)
  • Delivery
  • Business Unit Leads (Strategy & Ops)

Register your team today on the Pitch registration page or Hack registration page. The deadline for Pitch team registration is Wednesday, August 8 at 11:00am ET, and the deadline for Hack team registration is Friday, August 10 at 8:00am ET.

Judges

  • Alex Reynolds, Senior Director, Platform Solutions
  • Marty Brodbeck, Chief Technology Officer
  • Sylvain Grande, GM, Motion
  • Donna Granato, GM, Editorial
  • Janet Giesen, GM, Enterprise
  • Rawson Daniel, GM, eCommerce Image

Judging Categories

Hacks should be customer focused, improving their experience with Shutterstock as a seller or buyer. Hacks will judged broadly across our business units:

  • eCommerce Image
  • Motion
  • Enterprise
  • Editorial
  • Shutterstock Custom

    • We'll also have a special category for best Hack for Shutterstock Editor.

Each submission is only eligible to win in one category. Note: judges may move hacks to a different category they feel is more appropriate.

After Hack demos finish on Friday, August 10, judges will deliberate and then announce the winners via live stream the same day.

Prizes:

The winning teams will receive a prize (details TBD) and their hack/pitch will be considered for a sprint in 2018-19.
